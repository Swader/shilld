Shilld: Visible PAID SHILL badges on X/Twitter

The Shilld extension adds a bold PAID SHILL badge to tweets and profile headers for usernames from a public list.

  • Fetches the canonical list from /shills/_all.json (this site).
  • Background service worker caches results for 5 days.
  • Works on x.com and twitter.com, including dynamic timelines.
  • No data leaves your browser.
Browse directory How to install

How it works

A background service worker fetches the usernames list from this site (CORS-safe in the extension context), caches it for 5 days in chrome.storage, and the content script inserts the badge next to usernames in tweets and on profile headers.
